Introduction

ChartLync's goal is to simplify managing of medical records, vitals, appointments, insurance, secure record sharing and much more for everyone.
At ChartLync™ everything we do, we believe in challenging the status quo. We start that by putting patient at the center of care, providing very flexible, simple and user friendly application. We just happen to build great health information exchange software.
ChartLync™ provides collaborative care by securely exchanging clinical information with other organizations always with patient consent. ChartLync™ gives instant, secure access and enables sharing of information between various clinical systems across the globe in a snap at fraction of cost.

We help you effortlessly manage your health records in one place. The more accounts you link, the more you can do for youself.
